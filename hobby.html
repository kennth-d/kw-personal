Dragon Ball FighterZ

Dragon Ball FighterZ is a fast-paced 2.5D fighting game. It features iconic characters from the Dragon Ball series in intense 3v3 tag-team battles. The game blends accessible controls with deep mechanics, offering flashy combos, cinematic super moves, and high-level competitive play, all wrapped in a visually striking art style that closely mirrors the anime.

Assists

One of the core mechanics of the game allows you to call out your other characters breifly to attack, or in some cases defend.
